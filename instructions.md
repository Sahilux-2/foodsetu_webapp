We are building a web app version of the mobile app called "Food Setu".
There is a sign up/log in screen. Signup as volunteer or signup as NGO. And an “already have an account? Login” text link.
If user signs up as Volunteer then the screen asking for username, email and mobile number for OTP verification.
If user signs up as an NGO, then the screen would ask for name of NGO, email, contact number and a verification certificate to upload in pdf format.
After completing any of the 2 screens (volunteer or NGO) then a terms & conditions screen would come and a checkbox for the user to click on to activate the primary CTA and click on it to proceed forward in the flow.
After clicking on the primary CTA of the terms & conditions screen, the home screen comes in. The home screen consists of a search bar, location of the NGO or volunteer, filters (near me, veg, non-veg, rating 4.5+ and less than Rs. 600/kg), then 2 sections with college cards within each section.
The search bar gets activated when clicking on it and shows the recent college messes the user (NGO or volunteer) has searched for before, for FTUX, just an activated search bar.
Clicking on any college card a detailed view of the food available in that college’s mess is displayed. With the college name, distance from user location, rating and veg/non veg tag. Then the meal of the day sub heading with a card showing the meals in kgs and the image of that meal and a primary CTA.
On clicking the primary CTA, the checkout screen comes in. With segmented tabs for “Self pickup”  & “Delivery”. And other data points that I will show you in the mobile screen UI.
Then an order successfully placed screen with a green tick mark illustration.
